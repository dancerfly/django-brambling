Dancerfly is an event platform built by dancers, for dancers.

Leave registration to us—go make the rest of your event amazing!

Use Dancerfly for workshops, exchanges, series classes, & more.

Email us to get started!

Easy checkout

Zero account creation, plus a human-friendly design and checkout process! Use credit cards, Dwolla, and/or checks, whatever is easiest for you and your attendees!

Easy data

Get the data you need for your attendees and your event! We support custom forms, custom data downloads, and full financial reports, making the event management process as simple as possible for you!

Easy teams

Have a team of people involved in your event? Great! Control their access to data and editing on a per-event basis, or give them access to all your events at once!

We also support custom discount codes, simple housing management, easy refunds, and more!

Looking for a feature you don't see? Email us! If it's not there right now, we'll talk about adding it for you. We're always looking for ways to make Dancerfly even better!

Dancerfly is free for free events!

For everyone else, we add a fee of 1.5% to credit and Dwolla transactions.

Stripe

Credit card processing is handled through Stripe. Sensitive data is stored on their secure servers, and attendees don't have to leave our site or create any accounts!

2.9% + 30¢ on all transactions

Dwolla

Dwolla is an alternative payment service, available within the US. Attendees can pay with their Dwolla balance or directly from their bank account. On the plus side, the fees for Dwolla are dirt cheap – but they do require that attendees create a Dwolla account first.

25¢ for transactions over $10

Check

Since some attendees may not want or be able to process payments online, we also support payment by check, free of charge. Organizers can mark payments received in the management interface as they come in.

Free of charge!

You can use any or all of the above payment methods for your events. We'll never invoice you for payment; our portion (if any) is automatically taken out during payment processing and reported in your finances tab! Learn more about pricing
